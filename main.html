Game Overview

FIFA is a series of association football video games developed and released annually by Electronic Arts under the EA Sports label. It is one of the best-selling video game franchises.

History: The first game, FIFA International Soccer, was released in 1993. Since then, it has grown into a global phenomenon.

Gameplay: Players control teams and compete in matches, with realistic graphics and gameplay mechanics that emulate real-world football.
